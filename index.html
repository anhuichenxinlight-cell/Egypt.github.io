About CX Lighting

Leading LED Lighting Manufacturer in China, Committed to Creating Value for Global Partners

Anhui Chenxin Lighting Electrical Appliance Co., Ltd.

Headquartered in Anhui, China, we are a large-scale professional lighting manufacturer with 20 years of solid industry experience, integrating R&D, production and sales. As a leading domestic LED lighting manufacturer, we have a complete industrial chain from core component production to finished product delivery.

Our products are exported to more than 60 countries and regions including the United States, Brazil, Mexico, Germany, Spain, the UK, South Africa, Iran, Israel, Pakistan, etc., through online B2B platforms (Made-in-China, Global Sources, Alibaba) and offline physical sales channels.

Our mission: Creating value for clients, Offering opportunities to staff, Bringing wealth to society. We look forward to achieving win-win success with all partners through our superior products, reputation and services.
